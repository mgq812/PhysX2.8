NxVec3 NxBoxShapeDesc::dimensions
 

Dimensions of the box.

The dimensions are the 'radii' of the box, meaning 1/2 extents in x dimension, 1/2 extents in y dimension, 1/2 extents in z dimension. All three must be positive.

Range: .x (0,inf) Range: .y (0,inf) Range: .x (0,inf) Default: Zero
