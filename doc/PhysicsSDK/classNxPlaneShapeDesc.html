NxVec3 NxPlaneShapeDesc::normal
 

Plane normal. (unit length!).

The plane equation takes the form: normal.x * X + normal.y * Y + normal.z * Z = d

Note: The plane equation defines the plane in world space. Any other transformations, like actor global pose and shape local pose are ignored by the plane shape.

Range: direction vector
Default: 0.0, 1.0, 0.0
